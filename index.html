01 / About

The Story So Far

I'm a full-stack developer in training, currently enrolled in a government-backed technology programme in Enugu, Nigeria, run in partnership with Turing Tech LLC. My programme covers HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, version control, and AI data annotation.

Before code, I had three laptops and a lot of time to figure out what I actually wanted to build. That journey taught me patience, resourcefulness, and the habit of pushing through problems until something finally works. Those traits show up in every project I touch.

My goal is to grow from a solid JavaScript foundation toward full-stack proficiency — and eventually into lower-level programming. Right now I'm documenting that journey publicly through my #BiP60 Build in Public challenge.
